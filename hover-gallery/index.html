Manga you should read at least once in your life:

Chainsaw Man

Chainsaw Man follows Denji, a young man burdened by debt who merges with his dog-like devil companion, Pochita, to become the hybrid Chainsaw Man. He joins the Public Safety Devil Hunters to fight devils threatening Japan, navigating a world of violence, power, and his own simple desires.

Dandadan

Dandadan is a manga series about Momo Ayase, a girl who believes in ghosts, and her classmate Ken Takakura, nicknamed "Okarun," who believes in aliens. After a bet to prove each other wrong, they encounter supernatural forces and awaken hidden powers, leading them to team up and fight against both aliens and spirits, all while navigating their evolving romantic feelings.

D.Gray Man

D.Gray-man is a dark fantasy manga and anime series where Allen Walker, a young exorcist, fights against the Millennium Earl and his army of Akuma, demonic weapons created from the souls of the dead. The series explores themes of despair, loss, and the complexities of good and evil, as Allen and other exorcists of the Black Order strive to protect humanity and find a powerful artifact called Innocence.

Gokurakugai

Gokurakugai is a Japanese manga series about a diverse working-class district where Tao and Alma, troubleshooters, accept various cases, including those involving supernatural elements and missing persons. They navigate the hidden darkness of the district while dealing with Magas (demons) and other challenges. The series explores themes of prejudice, social issues, and the blurred lines between good and evil.
